Defending Your Inbox with AI

InboxGuard is an advanced phishing detection tool that leverages the power of Google's Gemini AI to identify and neutralize sophisticated email threats.

The Growing Phishing Threat

Phishing attempts have increased by 300% since the COVID-19 pandemic began.

Today's phishing attacks are more sophisticated than ever before. Cybercriminals are using advanced tactics to bypass traditional security systems, including:

  • Basic brand impersonation and spoofed sender addresses
  • Context-aware attacks that reference real information about the target
  • AI-generated content that avoids traditional security flags

Even security-conscious users can fall victim to these advanced phishing attempts. InboxGuard was created to provide an additional layer of protection by analyzing content that traditional email security might miss.

Our Solution: AI-Powered Phishing Detection

InboxGuard combines traditional security techniques with cutting-edge artificial intelligence to provide comprehensive protection against phishing.

How InboxGuard Works

A behind-the-scenes look at our technology stack and architecture

1. Email Content Analysis

When you paste an email into InboxGuard, we extract key components like sender information, subject, body content, and embedded links. This parsed content is then prepared for AI analysis.

Python Email Parsing Natural Language Processing

2. Google Gemini AI Processing

The extracted email content is sent to Google's Gemini AI via API. Gemini analyzes the text for linguistics patterns, urgency indicators, threat language, and manipulation tactics common in phishing attempts.

Gemini AI Google API Function Calling

3. URL Analysis & Extraction

InboxGuard identifies all URLs in the email content and performs multiple checks for suspicious patterns. URLs are evaluated for domain spoofing, shorteners, suspicious TLDs, and other red flags.

URL Parsing Regex Pattern Matching Domain Analysis

4. Risk Scoring & Visualization

Results from the AI analysis and URL checks are combined to generate a comprehensive risk assessment. The findings are presented with visual indicators, detailed explanations, and specific warning indicators.

Risk Algorithm UI Visualization Reporting

5. Safe Link Preview

InboxGuard provides a sandboxed environment to safely preview suspicious links without exposing your system to potential threats. This helps users verify the legitimacy of links before clicking.

Sandbox Technology Iframe Isolation Web Preview
